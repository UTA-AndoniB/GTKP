# Purpose

This document defines the official terminology used across the Guardian Tales Knowledge Platform (GTKP).

Every document, schema, CSV file, JSON object, source code file, recommendation engine and interface must use these terms consistently.

The objective is to eliminate ambiguity and establish a common language for contributors.

---

# General Terms

## Hero

A playable character that can participate in one or more game modes.

---

## Exclusive Weapon (EX Weapon)

A unique weapon designed for a specific Hero.

An Exclusive Weapon may provide additional skills or effects that are unavailable with other weapons.

---

## Weapon

An equippable item that determines the Hero's attack attributes.

---

## Accessory

An equipment item that grants defensive or offensive bonuses.

---

## Shield

A defensive equipment item available for compatible Heroes.

---

## Card

An equipment enhancement that grants additional attributes.

---

## Relic

A special equipment obtained through Expedition.

Relics provide passive bonuses and set effects.

---

## Merchandise (Merch)

An equipment item crafted in the Workshop.

Merch provides passive bonuses and special abilities.

---

## Collection

A permanent account bonus unlocked by acquiring or upgrading game assets.

---

## Account

A player's complete game profile.

---

## Inventory

The collection of all assets owned by an Account.

---

## Team

A group of Heroes used together in a specific game mode.

---

# Hero Attributes

## Element

Represents the elemental affinity.

Possible values:

- Basic
- Fire
- Water
- Earth
- Light
- Dark

---

## Class

Represents the combat class.

Possible values:

- Warrior
- Ranged
- Tank
- Support

---

## Party Buff

The passive bonus granted to the entire team.

---

## Chain Skill

The Hero's chain attack activated after specific crowd-control conditions.

---

## Leader

The Hero currently controlling the team.

---

## Awakening

The Hero's Awakening Tree progression.

---

## Blessing

The Blessing system progression.

---

## Limit Break

The enhancement that increases the Hero's maximum level.

---

## Ascension

Special evolution available only for selected Heroes.

---

## Evolution

The star progression of a Hero.

Possible values:

- ★1
- ★2
- ★3
- ★4
- ★5
- ★6

---

# Equipment Terms

## Level

Current enhancement level.

---

## Limit Break

Additional enhancement beyond the maximum level.

---

## Option

Primary equipment attribute.

---

## Sub Option

Secondary equipment attributes.

---

## Exclusive Effect

Special passive effect available only on Exclusive Weapons.

---

# Progression

## Investment

Resources required to maximize a Hero.

---

## Priority

Relative importance of investing resources.

Possible values:

- Very High
- High
- Medium
- Low

---

## Build

A recommended equipment configuration.

---

## Synergy

Positive interaction between Heroes.

---

## Counter

A Hero capable of reducing another Hero's effectiveness.

# Naming Rules

All internal identifiers must use English.

Examples:

Hero

Weapon

Accessory

Relic

Inventory

Recommendation

Never mix English and Spanish names inside the source code.
